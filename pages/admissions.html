Admission Requirements

  • Completed application form
  • Birth certificate (original and copy)
  • Previous school report card or transcript
  • Two passport-sized photographs
  • Transfer letter (for transferring students)
  • Completed medical form

Application Process

1

Submit Application

Complete and submit the application form along with required documents.

2

Entrance Assessment

Eligible candidates will be invited for an entrance assessment and interview.

3

Admission Offer

Successful candidates receive an admission offer letter with enrollment details.

4

Enrollment

Complete enrollment by paying fees and submitting final documents.
